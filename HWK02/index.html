Why Audacity?

Audacity provides powerful tools for recording, editing, and exporting audio across Windows, macOS, and Linux. Its interface is designed to be approachable for beginners while remaining flexible for advanced users.

Accessibility and usability are core values of the platform, making it a reliable choice for a wide range of creative workflows.
